Personal Information

I am working with Jun Liu as a post-doc in Department of Statistics at Harvard University. I graduated from University of Georgia supervised by Wenxuan Zhong . I received a M.S. and B.S. in statistics supervised by Yaning Yang at University of Science and Technology of China.


Research Interests

Statistics: minimax nonparametric testing, smoothing spline, dimension reduction, controlled variable selection

Machine learning: neural network compression, variable selection via neural network

Computational biology: metagenomics, single cell, epigenomics, neuroimaging
