Personal Information

I am working with Jun Liu as an post-doc in Department of Statistics at Harvard University. I graduated from University of Georgia supervised by Wenxuan Zhong . I received a M.S. in statistics at University of Science and Technology of China, where I was supervised by Yaning Yang . My research lies in statistical theory&method, machine learning, and computational biology.


Research Interest

Statistical theory&method: non-parametric inference, minimax nonprametric testing, penalized likelihood ratio test, tensor product RKHS, smoothing spline, tensor dimension reduction

Deep learning: nerual network compression, variable selection via neural network

Computational Biology: metagenomics, epigenomics, neuroimaging
